At the Zen Dragon Academy, dragons are classified by their elemental affinity, reflecting their deep connection to nature. Fire dragons learn discipline and inspiration, water dragons master empathy and harmony, earth dragons cultivate resilience and protection, air dragons embrace freedom and agility, aether dragons seek cosmic understanding, and void dragons confront the mysteries of existence.
